# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated**: January 2025

## Introduction

Rubik Solver ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application ("App").

## Information We Collect

**We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.**

Rubik Solver is designed with privacy in mind. The app operates entirely on your device and does not:

- Collect personal data
- Store user information
- Transmit data to external servers
- Track user behavior
- Use analytics or tracking services
- Access your contacts, photos, or other personal files

## Local Data Storage

All cube configurations and solving data are stored locally on your device. This includes:

- Current cube state
- Solution steps
- User preferences

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third parties.

## Third-Party Services

Rubik Solver does not integrate with any third-party services that collect personal information. The app uses:

- **Flutter Framework**: Open-source UI framework (no data collection)
- **cuber Library**: Local cube solving algorithms (no data collection)
- **flutter_scene**: Local 3D rendering (no data collection)

## App Permissions

Rubik Solver does not require any special permissions beyond basic app functionality. We do not request access to:

- Camera
- Microphone
- Location
- Contacts
- Photos
- Files
- Network access (except for app updates via app stores)

## Children's Privacy

Rubik Solver is safe for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13, and since we don't collect any information, this is not a concern.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

## Your Rights

Since we don't collect any personal data, there's no data to access, modify, or delete. You have complete control over the app and its local data.
